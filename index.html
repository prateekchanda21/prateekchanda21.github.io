Prateek Chanda

Ph.D. Student in CSE, IIT Bombay

I work on efficient large-scale machine learning with a focus on language models, distillation, continual learning, and principled subset selection.

I am advised by Ganesh Ramakrishnan and Pratik Jawanpuria, and supported by the Prime Minister Research Fellowship (2022-2027) and the Microsoft PhD Award (2025-2026).

I have worked as a Student Researcher at Google DeepMind India (on efficient pretraining and distillation for Gemini and LaMDA), and interned at Microsoft Research India on generalizable test-time scaling.

Before my Ph.D., I was a Research SWE (SCAI Fellow) at Microsoft Research India and completed my undergrad at IIEST Shibpur. During undergrad, I contributed to SunPy with NASA GSFC collaborators and received the NASA Group Achievement Award.

Research Directions

  • Efficient pretraining and fine-tuning of large language models
  • Continual learning with constrained adaptation in LLMs
  • Robust distillation and representation compression
  • Submodular subset selection, coresets, and scalable optimization
  • Federated and personalized learning
