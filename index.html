Prateek Chanda - Home Page

 

Email : prateekkol21@gmail.com, prateekch@cse.iitb.ac.in

I am a 3rd 4th year Ph.D. student at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, CSE Department advised by Ganesh Ramakrishnan, working on large scale efficient machine learning, generously supported by the Prime Minister Research Fellowship (2022-2027) and the Microsoft PhD Award (2025-2026). I have been a part-time Student Researcher at Google Deepmind India hosted by Pradeep Shenoy during (July 2023-May 2024) working on Efficient Pretraining of Large Language Models and Knowledge Distillation for Large Language Models. During my time at Deepmind, I had the pleasure of working with and being mentored by Dheeraj Nagaraj, Arun Suggala and Prateek Jain.

I am also collaborating with Dr. Krishnateja Killiamsetty at IBM Almaden Research Labs.

Trajectory

Before Ph.D., I was a research software engineer (SCAI Fellow) at Microsoft Research India Bangalore, hosted by Amit Sharma and Vageesh Chandramouli. Before this I completed my undergrad at IIEST Shibpur where I was advised by Prof. Susanta Chakrabarty (former Dean) and Prof. Asit Kr. Das.
