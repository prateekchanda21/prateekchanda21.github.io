🌎 Travels

NeurIPS 2025 — San Diego, California, USA

📅 December 6–7, 2025

Attended NeurIPS 2025 in San Diego — one of the premier machine learning conferences. Beyond the conference, got a chance to explore the beautiful coastal city, including the Gaslamp Quarter, La Jolla, and Torrey Pines.

ICLR 2024 — Vienna, Austria

📅 May 2024

Attended ICLR 2024 in Vienna — one of the top conferences in machine learning and AI. Vienna was a spectacular host city — stunning imperial architecture, world-class museums, coffee house culture, and the beautiful Danube.

Morocco Road Trip — Tetouan, Asilah & Casablanca

📅 May 1–6, 2026

A road trip through northern Morocco — exploring the walled medina of Tetouan (a UNESCO World Heritage Site), the artistic Atlantic coastal town of Asilah, and ending at Casablanca. Stunning mix of Andalusian architecture, Atlantic coastline, and vibrant street life.
